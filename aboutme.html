About me

I’m a freelancer Green UX/UI designer who loves to work remotely, from wherever the world is calling. I’ve been on the road for quite some years and I’m in love with my freedom, my yoga mat and the ocean. My journey to become a UX designer starts from my background in sustainable business, when I realized that also and maybe especially technologies and digital products can help transforming the world in a greener world. Because of this, my work as a designer is often inspired by planet earth, with organic designs and natural colors. My vision is to work with you to create a climate friendly web with more sustainable products, satisfied and happy users and healthy communication.
